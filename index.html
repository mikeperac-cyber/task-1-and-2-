Reading Test

Passage 1: The Honeybee

"The queen is the only fertile female and her sole purpose is to lay eggs, up to 2,000 a day during the spring. Worker bees are sterile females who perform all the other tasks in the hive, including foraging for nectar..."
1. The queen bee is responsible for foraging for food.
2. Worker bees are male insects.
3. A queen bee lays exactly 2,000 eggs every single day of the year.
⚠️ Trap Alert! The answer is FALSE. The text says "up to" (a maximum, not exactly) and "during the spring" (not every day of the year). IELTS changes "some" to "all" or "up to" to "exactly" to trick you!
4. Nurse bees are the oldest bees in the hive.
5. The waggle dance is used to communicate the location of food.

✍️ Writing Task 1 — Band 7 Model Answer (Bar Chart)

The bar chart illustrates the proportions of Australian men and women who engaged in five distinct leisure activities in 2023.

Overall, it is clear that watching TV was the most popular pastime for both genders, while reading and playing sports showed the most significant gender differences. Generally, men preferred more active or screen-based leisure, whereas women showed higher participation in reading and gardening.

Looking at the most popular activities, watching TV was highly favoured by both men (85%) and women (80%). Socialising was also common, with slightly more women (75%) participating compared to men (70%).

However, distinct gender preferences emerged in the remaining categories. Playing sports was considerably more popular among men (60%) than women (35%). Conversely, reading was much more prevalent among women (65%) compared to men (40%). Finally, gardening attracted a similar, moderate percentage of both men (45%) and women (50%).

👩‍🏫 Teacher's Guide for Gulsum (A1/A2 → Band 5 → 7)

  • Grammar: To reach Band 7, use complex sentences with words like Although, Because, While, Which.
    Example: "Although technology is useful, it can be stressful."
  • Vocabulary: Learn collocations (words that go together): "heavy rain" (not "thick rain"), "make a decision" (not "do a decision").
  • Exam Strategy: In Reading, if a question says "ALL" or "EVERY", it is usually FALSE. The text usually says "some" or "many". Always check details!
  • Writing Task 1: Always include an Overview paragraph (starting with "Overall,…"). Without it, you cannot score above Band 5.
